Amazing Brazilian Carnival

Carnival is the most famous holiday in Brazil and has become an event of huge proportions. It is an annual week long festival that unites all of Brazil and nearly brings the country to a hault.

Festivities are intense, day and night, mainly in coastal cities. Rio de Janeiro's carnival alone drew 4.9 million people in 2011, with 400,000 being foreigners.

Brazilian Soccer Facts

The Brazilian national team has many nicknames and are known in different parts of the world. Being Canarinho the most famous, meaning "Little Canary", a phrase that was popularized by the late cartoonist Fernando "Mangabeira" Pieruccetti during the 1950 World Cup.

Many distinguished commentators consider the 1970 brazilian squad to be the greatest association football team ever, although some argue the case for other teams.

Brazil is the only national team to have won the World Cup on four different continents: once in Europe (1958 Sweden), once in South America (1962 Chile), twice in North America (1970 Mexico and 1994 United States) and once in Asia (2002 Korea/Japan).
